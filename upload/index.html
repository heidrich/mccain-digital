whatever-recall — the code is its own memory.

Stop paying your AI to re-read the same files. recall is an AI-native project memory: knowledge is stamped onto the commit at write-time — while the AI still knows why. Reading it back takes microseconds and zero tokens.

Self-hosted

Runs entirely on your machine. No sync, no telemetry — your code never leaves your computer.

Can't go stale

Every note is pinned to a commit. The moment the code moves on, recall raises a flag.

Rules that stick

Standing instructions your AI checks before every edit — they can't be skipped or forgotten.
