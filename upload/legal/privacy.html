If you contact us via the contact form or by email, we process the data you provide (name, email address, message) exclusively to handle your inquiry (Art. 6 (1) lit. b GDPR). The data is deleted once it is no longer required for this purpose and no statutory retention obligations apply. The contact form is processed via Web3Forms (privacy policy) and our hosting provider SiteGround (privacy policy).
