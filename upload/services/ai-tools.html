01 — What we build

From a prompt to a product.

Four things, done properly — each one wired into your stack with the guardrails, monitoring and UI that turn a clever demo into something your team relies on every day.

RAG over your own data

Retrieval-augmented generation grounded in your documents, code, tickets or DB — with citations, so the model answers from your truth, not its training set. Vector search (pgvector, Qdrant) + re-ranking + a confidence/abstain path.

Autonomous & multi-step agents

Workflows that run themselves — plan, call your real APIs via tools, and check their own work before they hand it back. Built with guardrails, retries and human-in-the-loop where it matters, so automation is reliable, not a liability.

MCP tool-servers & integrations

We give models hands: Model Context Protocol servers and function-calling into your CRM, ERP, internal APIs and databases — with auth, rate-limits and audit logging. The model does real work in your systems, safely.

Automation that pays for itself

The repetitive work — triage, data entry, summarising, routing, drafting — handed to pipelines (n8n, custom loops, scheduled jobs) that run quietly in the background and save real hours every week.
